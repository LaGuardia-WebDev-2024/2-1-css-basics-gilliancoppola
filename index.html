European Hamster


The European Hamster is awesome because it is a little-known relative of domestic hamsters. Many people think that hamsters don't ever live in the wild, but these do. European hamsters are larger than domestic hamsters, being about the size of a guinea pig. They are native to grasslands and farmlands of Eurasia, foraging for food in the night and around sunset. They are nocturnal/crepuscular. European hamsters are endangered.
